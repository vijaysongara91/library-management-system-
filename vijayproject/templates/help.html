Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

To borrow a book, search for the book in the Search Books section, then click on the borrow button if the book is available. You may need to log in to your account to complete the borrowing process.
Go to the My Books page and click the Return button next to the book you want to return. Follow the instructions to confirm the return. Make sure to return the book by the due date to avoid any late fees.
Please contact the library staff immediately through the contact information below to report a lost book. You may be required to pay a replacement fee or provide a replacement copy depending on the library's policy.
You can renew a book by visiting the My Books page and clicking the Renew button if the option is available. Renewals are subject to availability and may not be allowed if the book is reserved by another user.
Navigate to the Profile page where you can edit and save your personal information such as your name, email, and contact details. Make sure to save changes before leaving the page.
